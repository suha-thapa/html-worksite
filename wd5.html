💄 Bronzy Glam Makeup Tutorial

🌟 Base (Flawless & Glowing)

  • Prep Your Skin: Use a hydrating moisturizer and glowy primer.
  • Foundation: Apply medium to full coverage dewy foundation with a beauty blender.
  • Concealer: Brighten under eyes, forehead, and chin, then blend.
  • Contour & Bronze: Cream contour your face, then layer with bronzer.
  • Blush: Use rosy or terracotta blush on the cheeks.
  • Highlighter: Highlight cheekbones, nose, cupid’s bow, and chin with golden tones.

👁️ Eyes (Defined & Soft Glam)

  • Eyebrows: Fill in with pencil and set with clear brow gel.
  • Eyeshadow: Blend warm brown in crease, shimmer on lids, deepen outer corners.
  • Eyeliner: Line upper lash line and create a mini wing.
  • Lashes: Curl lashes, apply mascara, and use falsies if desired.

💋 Lips (Matte & Defined)

  • Lip Liner: Outline lips with brown or mauve pencil.
  • Lipstick: Fill in with matte nude or mauve lipstick.
  • Optional Highlight: Add a touch of gloss/highlighter at the center of lips.

✨ Finishing Touches

  • Set your makeup with a dewy setting spray.
  • Add faux beauty marks with a brown eyeliner (optional).
