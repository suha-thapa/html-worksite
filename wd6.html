💖 Dewy Peach Makeup Look Tutorial

🌸 Base (Glowy and Fresh)

  • Prep: Start with a lightweight moisturizer and a hydrating primer.
  • Foundation: Use a light-to-medium coverage dewy foundation or a skin tint for a natural glow.
  • Concealer: Apply a minimal amount under the eyes and on any redness or blemishes.
  • Cream Blush: Use a peach or coral-toned cream blush on the apples of the cheeks and blend upwards.
  • Highlighter: Tap a cream or liquid highlighter on the cheekbones, nose tip, and brow bones.

👁️ Eyes (Soft Peachy Shimmer)

  • Brows: Keep them soft and brushed with a brow gel.
  • Eyeshadow: Use soft peach tones all over the lid and blend into the crease.
  • Shimmer: Add a light golden or champagne shimmer to the center of the lids and inner corners.
  • Lashes: Curl your lashes and apply a lengthening mascara. Falsies are optional but keep them natural.

💋 Lips (Glossy Nude Peach)

  • Lip Liner: Lightly line your lips with a nude or soft brown liner.
  • Lipstick: Use a peachy-nude creamy lipstick.
  • Gloss: Finish with a clear or peach gloss for a juicy lip effect.

✨ Final Touch

  • Setting Spray: Mist a dewy setting spray to lock in the glow.
  • Extras: Add soft peach tones to the nose for a sun-kissed look.
